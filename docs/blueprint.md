# **App Name**: Spark Vision Landing

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Dual-language (EN/AR) hero section with headline, subtext, tech illustration, and CTA buttons.
- About Us Section: Dual-language 'About Us' section describing Spark Vision.
- Services Section: Service cards with dual-language titles and icons for key services.
- Why Choose Us Section: Bulleted list of reasons to choose Spark Vision, presented in both English and Arabic.
- Portfolio/Case Studies Section: Display project cards with images and bilingual titles/descriptions.
- Testimonials Section: Client feedback section with bilingual names and quotes.
- Contact Section: Contact form with dual-language labels and a 'Send Message' button.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Blue (#005DFF) to convey trust and innovation.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) to ensure readability and a modern feel.
- Accent color: A slightly lighter blue (#3385FF) for interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for a clean and corporate aesthetic.
- Support both LTR (English) and RTL (Arabic) layouts for seamless bilingual experience.
- Incorporate smooth, subtle animations to enhance the modern, futuristic feel.
- Use professional, modern icons to represent services and key features.
